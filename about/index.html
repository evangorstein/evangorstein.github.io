About

Welcome to my website! I am a first year PhD student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I hope to use this site to put into writing various thoughts I have as I take courses and eventually perform research. Before graduate school, I lived in Chicago all my life, growing up in the suburbs and then attending the University of Chicago. Outside of school, I enjoy playing basketball, reading philosophy, and listening to music.
